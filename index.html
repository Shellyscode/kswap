FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is K-SWAP free to use?

+

100% free. No listing fees, no commission, no hidden charges. We built this for students, not for profit.

How do I know sellers are real KIIT students?

+

Every user signs up with a valid KIIT roll number. We perform roll number format validation at signup and restrict access to currently enrolled students only — alumni accounts are not permitted. However, please note that K-SWAP does not independently verify enrollment status with KIIT University — exercise your own judgement when transacting. Look for the ✓ badge on profiles.

What is bartering and how does it work?

+

Bartering means trading items without money. For example, you can trade your 3rd semester books for someone's calculator. When listing an item, select "Barter" and describe what you want in exchange.

Where do we meet to exchange items?

+

Always meet in public campus locations — canteens, library lobbies, campus gates. Each listing shows a suggested meetup spot. Never go to someone's room alone.

What if someone scams me?

+

Since every user is a verified KIIT student, scams are rare and traceable. You can report any user, and we'll take action. Always check seller ratings before trading. If something goes wrong, email us at kswap.kiit@gmail.com.

Can alumni use K-SWAP?

+

Currently, K-SWAP is for active KIIT students only. We may open it to alumni in the future for mentorship and giving away items to juniors.

How is K-SWAP different from OLX or Facebook Marketplace?

+

K-SWAP is exclusively for KIIT students. Every user is verified. Meetups happen on campus. There are no random strangers. Plus, we have bartering — something no other platform offers for campus trade.

FAQ & Help Center

Everything you need to know about K-SWAP

Is K-SWAP free to use?

+

100% free. No listing fees, no commission, no hidden charges.

How does signup work?

+

Enter your KIIT roll number (currently enrolled students only — alumni accounts are not permitted) and your personal email (Gmail, etc.). We send a 6-digit OTP to your personal email to verify access. Note: we perform roll number format validation only and do not independently verify your enrollment with KIIT University.

What can I sell on K-SWAP?

+

Anything campus-related: textbooks, lab coats, calculators, cycles, room accessories, study notes, clothing, gaming gear, and more. No illegal or inappropriate items.

How does bartering work?

+

Instead of selling for money, you can trade your item for something else. Select "Barter" when posting and describe what you want in exchange. Chat with interested people to finalize the trade.

Where should I meet to exchange items?

+

Always meet in public campus locations like canteens, library lobbies, or campus gates. Each listing shows a suggested meetup spot. Never go to someone's room alone.

How do I report a bad seller or scam?

+

Tap the 🚩 Report button in any chat, or email us at kswap.kiit@gmail.com with screenshots. We review every report and take appropriate action. Always meet in public and inspect items before paying — K-SWAP does not mediate disputes or guarantee transactions.

Can I edit or delete my listing?

+

Go to your Profile → My Listings. You can manage all your active listings from there.

How are ratings calculated?

+

After each trade, both buyer and seller can rate each other (1-5 stars). Your profile shows the average of all ratings received. Higher ratings build trust!

Is my personal information safe?

+

We only show your name and school on your profile. Your roll number, email, and password are never shared. Data is stored securely on encrypted servers via Supabase.
